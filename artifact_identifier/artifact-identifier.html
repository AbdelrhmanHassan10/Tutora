Scanner FAQ

What kind of image quality is required? expand_more

For best results, please use a clear, well-lit photo where the artifact is the main subject. Avoid heavy shadows, reflections, or obstructions.

Is my data and photo kept private? expand_more

Yes. We are committed to your privacy. Images are processed anonymously and are not stored on our servers after the analysis is complete.

How accurate is the AI? expand_more

Tutora AI has a 97% accuracy rate for artifacts within our database. We are constantly updating our model with new archaeological discoveries.
