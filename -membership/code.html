Membership FAQ

Can I upgrade my membership tier mid-year?expand_more

Yes! You can upgrade at any time. The remaining balance of your current tier will be applied toward the higher tier's annual fee.

Are memberships transferable or refundable?expand_more

Memberships are non-transferable but may be refunded within 30 days of purchase if no benefits have been used.

Do children need their own membership?expand_more

Children under 12 are admitted free with any active member. A Family Add-On ($75/year) covers up to 3 children aged 12-17.

What happens when my membership expires?expand_more

You'll receive renewal reminders 30 and 7 days before expiration. Early renewal within 60 days of expiry preserves your membership anniversary date.
