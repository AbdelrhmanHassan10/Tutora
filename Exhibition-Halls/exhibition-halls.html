Sequential Discovery

The Main Galleries

01-03

Dawn & Old Kingdom

Explore the origins of society and beliefs. Witness the mysterious Reserve Head and the protective Ptah-Sokar-Osiris figure from the age of the Pyramid Builders.

04-06

The Middle Kingdom

A period of cultural renaissance. Witness the majestic Statue of Senusret I and explore the daily lives, royal power, and spirituality of a reunified Egypt.

07-09

The New Kingdom

The golden age of empire. Witness the Victory Stele, the exquisite Shabti Box, and the Papyrus of Ani alongside the warrior statues of Thutmose III.

10-12

Late & Greco-Roman

The transformation of Egypt through the Third Intermediate Period, culminating in the cultural synthesis of the Greco-Roman era and the Pompey's Pillar.

The Living Sun King

Colossus of
Ramses II

Standing 11 meters tall and weighing 83 tons, the 3,200-year-old red granite statue of Ramses II welcomes every visitor. Standing exactly where the sun rays illuminate it twice a year.

The Great Atrium
Atrium Masterpieces

Guardians of
The Great Hall

Beyond the colossal Ramses, the Grand Hall houses treasures that define the spiritual and military history of Egypt.

The Ptolemaic Royal Pair

These monumental colossi of a Ptolemaic King and Queen were retrieved from the depths of the Mediterranean. Standing side-by-side in the Grand Hall, they guard the entrance with a unique blend of Hellenistic artistry and ancient Egyptian divine authority.
