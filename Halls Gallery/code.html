Masterpiece Gallery

Tutankhamun's Treasure

For the first time since their discovery in 1922, the complete collection of King Tutankhamun—over 5,000 artifacts—is displayed in one immersive space. Spanning two massive galleries, the collection reveals the life, death, and divinity of the Boy King.

The Golden Mask

The eternal face of the king, crafted from 11kg of solid gold and lapis lazuli.

Celestial Throne

An intricate masterpiece of wood, gold, and semi-precious stones symbolizing royal power.
