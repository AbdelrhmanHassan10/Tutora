Overview

Welcome to the Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM). By accessing our website, purchasing tickets, or visiting our physical premises, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. These terms constitute a legally binding agreement between you and the Grand Egyptian Museum Authority.

Our mission is to preserve and showcase the heritage of ancient Egypt. To ensure a safe and respectful environment for all visitors and the preservation of our artifacts, we require compliance with all regulations stated herein.

Ticket Usage

All tickets are subject to availability and are valid only for the date and time slot specified. Tickets are non-transferable and non-refundable, except in cases where the Museum is forced to close due to unforeseen circumstances.

  • check_circle Visitors must present a valid digital or printed ticket at the entrance.
  • check_circle Reduced-price tickets require valid government-issued identification.
  • check_circle The Museum reserves the right to refuse entry to unofficial tickets.

Code of Conduct

To protect the antiquities and ensure the comfort of all patrons, visitors must adhere to the following rules:

do_not_touch

Do not touch any artifacts or displays.

Flash photography is strictly prohibited.
