Visitor Help & FAQ

search
How do I book tickets? expand_more

Tickets can be booked directly through our official website or at the museum's visitor center. We highly recommend booking in advance, especially for the Tutankhamun gallery.

Book Now
Can I gift a ticket to someone else? expand_more

Yes, tickets are not tied to a specific name. You can forward the QR code to a friend or family member, and it will be valid for a single entry on the specified date.

What are the opening hours? expand_more

The Grand Egyptian Museum is open daily from 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Last entry is at 8:30 PM. Some special exhibitions may have slightly different hours.

Are there guided tours in multiple languages? expand_more

Yes, we offer professional guided tours in English, Arabic, French, German, Spanish, and Mandarin. You can also use our **AI Tour Guide** app for a self-paced experience.

Is there a café or restaurant on site? expand_more

GEM features multiple dining options, ranging from quick snacks to fine dining with views of the Pyramids. Visit our Dining page for details.

Is photography allowed? expand_more

Personal photography (no flash) is allowed in most areas. Tripods and professional equipment require a special permit obtained at the Media Office.

Can I bring liquids or food inside? expand_more

For the preservation of our artifacts, food and open drink containers are not allowed in the gallery spaces. You may enjoy them in the courtyard or at our restaurants.

Are there accessibility facilities? expand_more

Yes, GEM is fully accessible with ramps, elevators, and assistive technologies. We also offer complimentary wheelchairs at the Visitor Center.

Where is the Lost & Found? expand_more

The Lost & Found office is located near the main Information Desk in the Great Hall. Our staff will be happy to assist you in locating missing items.
