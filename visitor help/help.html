Visitor Help & FAQ Center

search
How do I book tickets? expand_more

Tickets can be booked directly through our official website or at the museum's visitor center. We highly recommend booking in advance, especially for the Tutankhamun gallery and special exhibitions, as daily capacity is limited. Online bookings will receive a QR code via email for fast entry.

What are the opening hours? expand_more

The Grand Egyptian Museum is open daily from 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Please note that last entry to the galleries is at 8:30 PM. Special night tours are available on selected Fridays and Saturdays until midnight.

Is photography allowed? expand_more

Photography for personal use is permitted in most areas of the museum using smartphones or compact cameras. However, flash photography and tripods are strictly prohibited. In the Tutankhamun exhibition and certain sensitive areas, photography is not allowed to preserve the artifacts. Professional film crews must obtain prior written permission.

Are there discounts for students or seniors? expand_more

Yes, discounted tickets are available for students (with valid ID), seniors (60+), and children under 12. Local Egyptian residents also benefit from a special pricing tier. Please ensure you have valid identification ready at the entrance to verify your discount eligibility.
