Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my ticket date? add

Yes, date changes are possible up to 48 hours before your visit, subject to availability. Please contact ticketing@gem.gov.eg with your order number.

Is there a dress code for the museum? add

We recommend comfortable walking shoes and respectful attire. As a cultural site, modest clothing is appreciated.

Are there lockers for large bags? add

Yes, secure lockers are available at the Visitor Center for a small fee. Large backpacks and suitcases are not allowed inside the galleries.
